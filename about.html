Reinventing the future of laundry services in New York City.

CARMIQUE provides a reliable, speedy, and professional laundry service that adjust to you individual needs.

We collect, clean, and deliver your laundry to your doorstep. When and where you need us, we will be there. We aim for our standard laundry service to be delivered the next day.

Founded in 2023 in a New York City shared apartment, CARMIQUE strives to be the premier laundry service provider in the New York City Metropolitan Area with goals of delighting customers as our focus. We offer professional laundry delivered to your doorstep in as quick as 24 hours.

We are committed to being good environmental stewards to ensure we have an enduring and resilient world. By using energy efficient machines, environmentally safe detergents, and reusable carrier bag to reduce waste, water & electric consumption.
